What do we do here at Anime Association?

It is no secret that a major percentage of those that watch Anime, especially those above their teen years, are considered strange, abnormal or downright weirdos in the eyes of the public. Even though this negative judgement and critisism from society was more prominent in the past than it is now, there are some who take an emotional and mental beating because of how society views them.

I created this site so that people like us have a safe haven to be ourselves when you cannot find it in you to be yourself in the outside world. On that note I say to hell with what people say about you in regards to anime!

It is also no secret that you got interested enough to visit my site because you either have a passing interest in anime, or are a huge fan and looking for some info. Either way you are welcome here, because this is where we all gather to share some info, pictures, videos, music or simply chat for gags!

To start, create an account and log in. Then, select or search for a topic, anime, group, or you can create one, then let the fun begin!
